The Standard

Most buns are a delivery vehicle. Soft, forgettable, in the way. We think the bun is half the bite — every batch slow-fermented, hand-shaped, and built to hold up to the serious cook who spent three days on the filling.

Classic Brioche

Rich, pillowy, and just sweet enough. Made with cultured butter and whole eggs from pasture-raised hens. The universal premium bun — pairs with everything from smash to lobster.

Dark Pretzel

Lye-dipped for that true crust. Deep brown, chewy, slightly bitter. Built for smash burgers, brats, and anything that deserves a sharp mustard and a serious bun to hold it.

Potato Milk
